Welcome to the diagnostic system for RevenuePilot. This structural workspace computes deep mathematical viability scores across critical product engineering boundaries. Learn how to accurately configure inputs below:

1. Global Environmental Boundary Vectors

Total Addressable Market (TAM)

The total global or regional demand outline for this software category if competition were fully minimized.

Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

The specific geographic, financial, or linguistic subset targeted directly by FaridEx platform tools.

Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)

Your short-term capture envelope. The baseline portion of the market your product channels can defend within a 24-month horizon.

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

Blended marketing, engineering traction, and conversion capital required to activate a single enterprise account node.
