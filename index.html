User Guide

What is the Lab?

FaridEx Labs is a digital testing space. It helps you check if a product or business idea is practical and financially viable before you spend time and money building it.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Go to Validation Tests

Click on Validation Tests at the top of the page to see the available tools.

2

Select Any Test

Look at the available choices and find an active test, such as RevenuePilot.

3

Read the Test Manual

Click the Test Manual button on that card to read the rules, what data is needed, and what results you will get.

4

Use the Tool

Close the manual and click Run Feasibility Test to open the sandbox and start typing in your numbers.
