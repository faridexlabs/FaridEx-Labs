RevenuePilot · Intelligence Engine

Purpose

Evaluates whether a product idea or feature has strong revenue potential before development investment. Helps product teams avoid building financially weak products by generating structured feasibility intelligence.

Input Parameters

💰 Pricing model 🎯 Target market size 📈 Estimated adoption rate 🛠️ Feature type ⚙️ Implementation cost 👥 Customer segment

Outputs & Scoring

Revenue Feasibility Score (0-100) ROI Confidence Score Monetization Probability Break-even Estimation (months) Revenue Risk Level (Low/Med/High)

Each score is derived from weighted metrics, market benchmarks, and simulated unit economics.

Primary Goal

Prevent teams from investing weeks/months into features with poor monetization potential. Quantifies financial risk and provides clear go/no-go signals. Ideal for product managers, startup founders, and SaaS leaders.

Part of Faridex Labs Ecosystem

RevenuePilot is the first module of a complete decision intelligence suite (FeatureImpact OS, ScopeRisk IQ, ChurnShield, PriorityMatrix AI, etc.) that turns product decisions into measurable, comparable intelligence.
